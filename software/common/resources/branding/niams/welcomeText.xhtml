The Osteoarthritis Initiative (OAI) is a multi-center, nine-year observational study of men and women, sponsored by the National Institutes of Health (part of the Department of Health & Human Services). The goals of the OAI are to help us better understand how to prevent and treat knee osteoarthritis, one of the most common causes of disability in adults.

This website contains the permanent image archive of the radiographs and magnetic resonance images acquired during this study. The website is made possible by the NBIA Image Archive system.

Available Images:

  • 72-month, 48-month, 36-month, 24-month, 12-month, baseline X-Ray and MR images (entire cohort)
  • 30-month MR images for subset of progression cohort (483 participants)
  • 18-month MR images for subset of progression cohort (287 participants)
