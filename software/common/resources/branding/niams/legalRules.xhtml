The Osteoarthritis Initiative (OAI) is a multi-center, nine-year observational study of men and women, sponsored by the National Institutes of Health (NIH; part of the Department of Health & Human Services).  The goals of the OAI are to help us better understand how to prevent and treat knee osteoarthritis, one of the most common causes of disability in adults. 

This website contains the permanent image archive of the radiographs (X-Rays) and magnetic resonance images (MRIs) acquired during the OAI study.  The National Cancer Institute (NCI) offers this image repository to encourage cross-disciplinary science.  Some of this data may already be published or in public domain.

You may browse, download, and use the data for human subject research, for scientific and educational purposes.  The NCI does not warrant or assume any legal liability or responsibility for accuracy, completeness or usefulness of information in this archive.
